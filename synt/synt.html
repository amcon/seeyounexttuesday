Actor Writer See You Next Tuesday Literally Everything

What is it?

“SYNT is taking the do-it-all female comedy movement from the screen to the stage. Picking up cues from power chicks like Tina Fey and Lena Dunham, Erica Vlahinos has been bringing her brand of in-your-face funny feminism to New York since 2014.
Tired of playing dirty whores, virginal wives and chubby best friends, Vlahinos shifted gears from one end of the table to the other to write complex female leads, challenge stereotypes and most importantly, make people laugh. Proving that women can be strong, sexy and fucking hilarious.”

Go team!

“Functioning under the premise that the most kick-ass, edgy and innovative art comes when worlds collide, half of the SYNT Team has been on Broadway and the other half is under 25. This unique pairing of experienced sophistication and Millennial angst has created a production company that pushes the envelope and entertains.”
